How We Started

HelloBox was born from a simple idea: transforming personal memories into gifts you can see, hear, and touch. We started in a small home workshop, hand-assembling mini blocks, printing photos, and adding a recording feature to create truly unique gift boxes. What began as a surprise for friends soon attracted attention for its warmth and creativity, inspiring us to launch HelloBox in Hong Kong and share this special experience with others.

HelloBox 的誕生源於一個簡單的想法：將個人回憶變成看得見、聽得到、摸得到的禮物。我們從家中的小工作坊開始，手工組裝迷你積木、打印照片，並加入錄音功能，打造獨一無二的禮盒。最初只是送給朋友的驚喜，卻因溫度和創意而受到關注，讓我們決定在香港推出 HelloBox，分享這份獨特的體驗。

The Team

YUI

CEO/Co-Founder

As the founder and CEO of HelloBox, YUI leads with a vision to blend creativity and technology into every handmade product. With a background in product design, tech and entrepreneurship, he oversees strategy, operations, and innovation to bring personalized, meaningful gifts to life.

作為 HelloBox 的創辦人兼行政總裁，YUI 以融合創意與科技為願景，帶領團隊打造每一件手工禮品。憑藉產品設計、科技及創業背景，他負責制定策略、管理營運及推動創新，致力將個人化、有意義的禮物呈現給大家。

FUNG

CMO/CO-FOUNDER

As Chief Marketing Officer, FUNG drives HelloBox’s brand storytelling, social presence, and customer engagement. With deep expertise in digital marketing and Hong Kong’s cultural trends, she ensures each campaign resonates with our local audience and builds long-term brand love.

作為首席市場官，FUNG 負責推動 HelloBox 的品牌故事、社交媒體曝光及客戶互動。憑藉對數碼營銷及香港文化潮流的深厚認識，她確保每個推廣活動都能與本地受眾產生共鳴，建立長遠的品牌認同。
